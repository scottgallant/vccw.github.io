# Saturday, May 4th, 2013
# 11AM to 6PM, [Brentwood Park](https://plus.google.com/105953711653254975745/about?gl=us&hl=en)

Art, music and more on tap at this year’s festival!

There’s no better way to enjoy a spring Saturday than by spending it at the
Violet Crown Festival. Admission is free.

The festival will feature local arts and crafts vendors, family fun,
children’s activities, live music, and refreshments.

Parking is limited, so attendees are encouraged to walk or cycle to the
festival.

For more information, contact Mona Prater, 512-450-1167, [beadornment@hotmail.com](mailto:beadornment@hotmail.com).
